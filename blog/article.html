August 2025 Release Highlights

We're excited to announce game-changing features coming this August that will transform how teams manage customer support. These updates focus on enhanced collaboration and granular control.

Multi-Admin Support

Delegate tasks and empower your team with our new multi-admin functionality:

  • Onboard unlimited support agents/admins
  • Assign conversations to specific team members
  • Maintain oversight while distributing workload
  • Enable team members to act on your behalf

This eliminates bottlenecks and ensures customers always get timely responses from the most qualified team member.

Advanced RBAC System

Our new Role-Based Access Control provides unprecedented flexibility:

  • Create custom roles with granular permissions
  • Define access levels for different support tiers
  • Restrict sensitive actions to authorized personnel
  • One-click role assignment for new team members

Enterprise-Grade Support Center

Together these features enable fully-functional support teams:

  • Scale support operations without complexity
  • Maintain security with least-privilege access
  • Reduce resolution time with smart delegation
  • Audit trails for all admin actions

Whether you have 5 agents or 500, our new system adapts to your organizational structure and security requirements.

Rollout Timeline

These features will be available to all enterprise plans starting August 1, 2025:

  • July 30: Documentation and training materials released
  • August 4: Early access for beta customers
  • August 10: General availability for all customers
